3D Printing as a Hobby

3D printing is a fascinating hobby that allows you to create physical objects from digital designs. It's a great way to bring your ideas to life and experiment with new materials and techniques.

As a student balancing CIS and Aviation, I find that 3D printing is the perfect bridge between digital precision and physical engineering. It requires a deep understanding of software settings and mechanical troubleshooting.

Future Hobby Content

Over the course of the term, I plan to expand this section to document my 3D printing journey. You can expect to see the following content:

  • Hardware Reviews: Insights into the printer and tools I use.
  • Project Gallery: Photos and descriptions of completed prints, from functional parts to decorative art.
  • Troubleshooting Guides: Tips on how to handle common issues like bed adhesion or filament jams.
